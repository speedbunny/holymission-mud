USAGE:  cast bolt <victim>
COST:   Variable by level and intelligence
DAMAGE: Variable by level and wisdom

DESC: The bolt spell can be used multiple times in one round.
      The damage by this spell comes from a shock delivered
      to the victim through the Jedis hand. If you are
      currently in fight, bolt will default to your opponent.
      Otherwise a target must be specified.

HINT: Do not stand in water while playing with electricity.
