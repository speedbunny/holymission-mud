USAGE:  cast cure <wounds || poison || alcohol>
COST:   Equal to HP healed for wounds or level of poison
             poison includes intoxication
DAMAGE: Heals 20% of max HP at a casting. Removes all poison,
             but removal causes some loss of HP.

DESC: The Jedi concentrates on his bodily functions to stop
      bleeding, mend tears, and expel poisons such as alcohol.

HINT: There is still no cure for a bleeding heart.
