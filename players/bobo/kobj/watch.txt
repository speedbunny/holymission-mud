The following commands are supported by your watch:

  * show time  : This show you the local time.
  * show date  : This shows you the local date.
  * show zone  : This shows you on which time-zone the watch is fixed.
  * turn wheel forward : set the time-zone +1
  * turn wheel backward: set the time-zone -1
  * helpwatch    : This page

  * legend : CET - Central European Time
  *          GMT - Greenwich Mean Time
  *          WET - Western Europen Time (=GMT)
