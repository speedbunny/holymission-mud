*********************************************************************
                        CURE CRITICAL WOUNDS       
*********************************************************************
Name   : Cure critical wounds
Level  : 16
Cost   : 70
Syntax : cast cure critical wounds <on <player>>

        This is the basic healing power of the druids.  It is
a great help to a druid in combat and also makes the druid a
popular friend to have around.  It cures most critical wounds.
