*********************************************************************
                              BARKSKIN
*********************************************************************
Name   : Barkskin
Level  : 4
Cost   : 50
Syntax : cast barkskin

        Barkskin is one of the standard druid spells.  It causes
your skin to harden like the bark of a tree, thus covering any
exposed part of your body not protected by armour.  It is a
big help in combat.
