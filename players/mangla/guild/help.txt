

Guide:
        The power and majesty of this place cannot be truly
felt without joining in its sacred ranks. To help you make your
decision on choosing I will tell you something about this guild
and its powers. Once a member of the guild you will be given
the option to use many great powers of the mind. Harnessed mental
energy allows you to project burst of energy from your fingertips,
persuade others into peace or leaving, assessing the value of
some items that may be hidden, or speeding your own recovery.
Judge wisely and choose on which side of the Force you wish.

"May the Force be with you"
