USAGE:  help <ability || guild || levels>
COST:   Free
DAMAGE: N/A
DELAY:  No Delay

DESC: A simple help file on the ability will be listed.
      It is your job to figure out the best way to use it.
      If you use guild then a brief description of the
      guild will be displayed. Levels produces a list
      of the guild levels and titles you may attain.

HINT: You will have to see a doctor for the help you
      REALLY need. Preferably a psychiatrist.
